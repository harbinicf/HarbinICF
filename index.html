About Us

Harbin International Christian Fellowship is an interdenominational fellowship comprised primarily of students from foreign countries who are studying in China. The fellowship includes students from various campuses in Harbin. The Fellowship has been in existence for more than 15 years and has evolved from students meeting in dorm rooms to a Church setting where we meet each Sunday morning. Presently we are meeting online via Sype and Zoom due to the pandemic

Fellowship

The Fellowship structure is made up of a General Coordinator, Sectional heads and FC leaders along with various church auxillary leaders. They are as follows:

  • General Coordinator: Pastor Stephen Yabo
  • Elders: Brother Ala; Brother Lambo; Sister Lambo, Sister Yabo and Sister Ala
